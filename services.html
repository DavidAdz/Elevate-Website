Services Offered

Psychosocial Counseling

We seek to better understand the interactions between your psychological and social needs. We will provide help to improve your overall wellbeing in your social environment. We will aim to enhance your coping skills and promote personal growth.

Educational Psychological Counselling

We help school children and youths with academic difficulties, through consultation, case formulation, assessment and intervention as it relates to their learning, behavioural, emotional, mental health, and personal development. Provides support and monitoring of progress for young people and vulnerable children especially children at risk for exclusion. Provides basic educational testing to ssess academic function.

Adolescent Trauma Counselling

Focus on young children, teens and young adults who are experiencing traumatic episodes of hurt from childhood unresolved issues.

Relationship Counselling

Helps married clients overcome challenges in their marriage/ relationship: communication, issues with intimacy, and resolving major conflicts.Premarital Counselling is also available.

Marriage Counselling

Helps families work through their challenges and conflicts as a whole. Issues include: integrating blended families, healing from divorce, and working through grief.

Group Counselling

Group counselling offers a supportive environment for clients to connect with others facing similar challenges. In group counselling, clients can share their experiences, receive support from peers, and learn new coping strategies.

Individual Counselling

Team Building/Soft Skills

Helping groups and organizations to improve communication, collaboration, productivity and a safe working environment; and achieve shared goals. Soft skills are essential to influence how people interact with others and how they navigate the work space.

Church Leadership

Equips church officers to better understand their role, function and responsibilities. Provides insight into leadership from the officer and the persons being served.

All services are offered online and in person by appointment. Same day appointments on a case by case basis.
